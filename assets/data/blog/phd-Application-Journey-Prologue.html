Why I Chose to Continue Along the Academic Path

This post is the beginning of a longer record of my PhD application journey. I do not want to present it as a dramatic story or a polished success narrative. Instead, I want to document the uncertainty, the small decisions, and the real thoughts that appear before any clear outcome exists.

For me, writing is a way to slow down, organize the noise in my mind, and leave an honest trace of where this journey actually began.

Thoughts on Studying and Living Abroad

At this stage, I am still standing at the entrance. I have questions about research direction, academic ability, future location, and whether I am truly prepared for the long path ahead.

What feels most real right now is not certainty, but a mixture of curiosity, hesitation, pressure, and hope.
