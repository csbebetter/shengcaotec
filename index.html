👋 Welcome to Sheng Cao's home page

I am a master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, working in the State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Manufacturing Equipment and Technology.

My research focuses on high-precision large-scale spatial localization and measurement, point cloud registration, multi-sensor perception, and robotics for industrial measurement systems.

I am especially interested in robust 6-DoF pose estimation, laser tracker aided measurement, distributed measurement systems, and practical point cloud processing pipelines for large-scale engineering scenarios.

🧭 Selected Projects

Large-scale measurement / Point cloud registration

Global Point Cloud Registration for Aircraft Skin

Developed a distributed measurement framework using a 6-DoF cooperative target for aircraft skin point cloud registration in large-scale measurement environments.

Under review at IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics with major revision.

Industrial inspection / Laser scanning

Pipeline Inspection and End-Face Measurement System

Led the development of a rotational line-laser scanning system for pipe inspection, including point cloud processing, elliptical-cylinder fitting, and pipe pairing logic.
